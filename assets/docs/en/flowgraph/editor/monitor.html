General

The “FG Monitor” is a useful tool for debugging your flowgraph during runtime.


It provides 3 main functionalities:

  • A toolbar with buttons for:

    • hiding the monitor and opening the WorldEditor.
    • stopping the project.

  • An overview over the active states in your flowgraph.

  • An overview of all logged events similar to the “Event Log” window in Flowgraph.

Enabling & Disabling

The Monitor can be easily toggled by clicking the Use Monitor checkbox in the toolbar of the “Main Window” in the Flowgraph Editor.
